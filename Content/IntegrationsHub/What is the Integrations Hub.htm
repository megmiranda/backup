What is the Integrations Hub?

The Integrations Hub is where you set up connectors to your various data sources and outputs. In this menu, you see Sources, Authentications, and Catalog.

Sources    In Sources, you can view and edit your various data sources and connections. You can specify where you want to pull data from a specific data connector and where in Treasure Data you want it to go as well as upload CSV or TSV files.

Authentications    In Authentications, you can view or edit existing data connectors. You can also upload CSV or TSV files here and create new transfers of data from existing connectors.

Catalog    In the Catalog, you can create and configure new data connectors for ingesting and activating data.
